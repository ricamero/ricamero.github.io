Introduction

The Flying Finn

A newsletter dedicated to Finland's contribution to the sport of long distance runnning. The publication discusses the country's history with the sport and reports on the accomplishments of its present-day athletes. If it has to do with Finland, and it has to do with long distance running, it has been covered by The Flying Finn.
