About Us

With locations in downtown San Luis Obispo and Morro Bay, Riley's Cafe is a seasonally-inspired cafe with thoughtfully crafted coffee + tea drinks, as well as pastries using ingredients local to our region.

Our pastries are made in house, and our carefully brewed coffee is from Penny Coffee Roasters.

The owners, sisters Dana and Grace are SLO natives, who moved back home in 2015 to start their business.
